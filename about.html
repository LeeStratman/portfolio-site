About

Personal

Lee Stratman is passionate about creating functionality for websites and developing trusting relationships with his clients. His love of learning and unquenchable curiosity push him to refine his skills. In college he studied physics and was on the men’s cross country and track teams. Later in his academic career he took a computer programming class that left him fascinated and fired up. He wanted to incorporate programming into his life professionally, and a few years later Stratman Web Development was born. Before becoming a web developer, Lee worked as a race timer for Eclipse Timing, where he combined his technological knowledge with his love of running and travel. When he’s away from his computer he enjoys volleyball, disc golfing, running, reading about science, and rocking out to his favorite band, Muse.
